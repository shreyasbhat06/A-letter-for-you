My Dearest,

These words have waited a long time to find their way to you. I sat by the window this evening, the amber light falling across the old wooden desk, and I thought of you — as I always do when the world grows quiet.

There are things too tender for speaking aloud, so I have pressed them gently into ink and paper, hoping they reach you the way a song carries across still water.

Please know that wherever you are, whatever season you find yourself in, you are thought of — deeply, warmly, and always.

Forever yours,
